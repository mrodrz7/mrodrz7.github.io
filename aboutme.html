I am a Software Engineer, raised in Somerville, MA and of Latino descent. Before becoming an Engineer, I had a background in Quality Assurance as a QA Engineer and outside of that world I have about 8 years of experience in Customer Service and Retail. My favorite technologies to use are Vue.js and React.js for client side, and Ruby on Rails for the back-end. I've built a few projects independently and with colleagues at General Assembly, as well as simple websites and blogs both on my own and with guidance of outside resources such as Codeacademy, W3Schools, and Stack Overflow.


I'm an alumni of Year Up Greater Boston, where I graduated in the Quality Assurance program and of General Assembly where I attended the Software Engineering Immersive. My favorite part about that two year journey into Software Development were all the professional relationships I established, great friendships I made, and abolishing my fear of networking! Now my mission is to start a lifelong career as a Software Engineer and continue to learn whenever I can.


Outside of programming, my hobbies include playing soccer with my friends, spending time with loved ones, FIFA on the PS4, and trying to be the best dad to my little girl!
